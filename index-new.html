How it works

Three steps. No uploads. No waiting.

1
Select your file
Pick a PDF from your device. It stays right where it is — in your browser's memory.
2
Process locally
WebAssembly handles the heavy lifting at near-native speed. No servers involved.
3
Download result
Get your file instantly. Close the tab and everything is gone — nothing stored anywhere.

Why we built this differently

Most online PDF tools upload your files to their servers for processing. That means your tax returns, contracts, medical records, and private documents pass through someone else's infrastructure. We think that's unnecessary. Every tool here runs using WebAssembly in your browser — the same technology that powers desktop apps, now running at near-native speed in a tab.

✓
No file uploads — processing happens on your CPU
✓
No accounts, no tracking, no cookies required
✓

✓
Open source — inspect the code yourself
✓
No file size limits — your device is the only limit
