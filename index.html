How it works

Three steps. No uploads. No waiting.

Why we built this differently

Most PDF tools upload your files to their servers. Your tax returns, contracts, and private documents pass through someone else's infrastructure. Every tool here runs using WebAssembly in your browser — near-native speed, zero server contact.

✓ No file uploads — processing on your CPU
